Common Questions

Are these templates legally valid?
Yes — they use standard legal language drafted by a licensed attorney. We recommend attorney review for high-stakes documents. For routine agreements, these templates are ready to use as-is.
What format are the files in?
All 40 templates are delivered as Microsoft Word (.docx) files, fully editable and compatible with Google Docs, Apple Pages, and LibreOffice.
Can I use these for my business or clients?
Personal and single-business use is included with every purchase. For commercial resale, law firm use, or white-labeling with multiple clients, contact us for a Professional License.
What states do these apply to?
All templates include governing law fields you fill in with your state. They follow general U.S. contract law. State-specific documents (wills, leases, DNRs, deeds) include notes on state variations — always verify your state's specific requirements.
Do bundle purchasers get new templates free?
Complete Library bundle purchasers receive all future templates free. Individual and category bundle purchasers receive free updates to the templates in their bundle.

Due to the instant digital download nature of our products, we do not offer refunds. If you have any issue with your download, contact us and we will resolve it immediately.
